Mine OBX

Mining earns OBX by running a full node that does the network's proof-of-work. It's the most private way to get OBX — no swap, no counterparty — and it helps secure the chain. Rewards are paid straight to this wallet.

How to mine — 3 steps

  1. Download the Obscura node for your platform.
  2. Start it with mining on, pointed at your wallet address:
    ./obscura-node --mine --mine-address …
  3. Leave it running. Mined OBX appears in the Wallet tab — press Sync.
