Your keys and every signature run entirely in your browser (WebAssembly) — nothing is ever sent to a server except already-signed transactions. Confidential amounts, stealth addresses.
Privacy tip. On the hosted site, every request is routed through one operator's node, so that operator (and the hosting provider) can see your IP, your balance scans, and your swap activity, which weakens on-chain privacy. Your recovery phrase is stored encrypted in this browser (passphrase-protected) — but clearing site data still erases it, so keep your 24-word backup. For full privacy and self-custody, download and run your own node.
Want full privacy? Run your own node and point this wallet at it — then no shared operator ever sees your IP, balance scans, or swap intent.
  1. Download the node and start it: ./obscura-node (serves this same wallet UI locally).
  2. Open the wallet from http://localhost:<port> instead of this hosted site — your seed and all requests stay on your machine.

Your XNO proceeds wallet

When you mine OBX, this node automatically posts OBX→XNO swap offers and sells your OBX for XNO. The XNO you receive lands in a real Nano account derived from your miner seed — it is yours, and it is recoverable anywhere from the recovery key below. Nobody else holds it; no custodian, no admin.
